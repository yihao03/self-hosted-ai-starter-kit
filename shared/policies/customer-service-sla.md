# Customer Service SLA & Support Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-01

## Response Times

| Channel        | Priority | First Response | Resolution Target |
|----------------|----------|----------------|-------------------|
| Email          | Critical | < 1 hour       | 4 hours           |
| Email          | High     | < 4 hours      | 24 hours          |
| Email          | Normal   | < 24 hours     | 72 hours          |
| Email          | Low      | < 48 hours     | 5 business days   |

## Priority Definitions

- **Critical**: Service completely down, data loss or corruption, security breach, payment processing failure
- **High**: Feature not working as documented, significant performance degradation, API errors affecting production
- **Normal**: Minor bugs, feature questions, account issues, billing inquiries
- **Low**: Feature requests, general questions, feedback, documentation improvements

## Support Scope

We provide support for:
- Installation and setup assistance
- Configuration and deployment
- Bug identification and troubleshooting
- API usage and integration questions
- Billing and account management

We do not provide support for:
- Custom code modifications
- Third-party integrations beyond documented APIs
- Issues caused by user modifications to the software

## Escalation Process

1. **Tier 1 (Support Agent)**: Handles FAQs, general troubleshooting, account issues, basic configuration help
2. **Tier 2 (Technical Support)**: Escalated when Tier 1 cannot resolve, complex technical issues, bug investigation
3. **Tier 3 (Engineering/Management)**: Escalated for critical issues, security concerns, product bugs, refunds

## When to Escalate

Escalate to a human agent when:
- The customer explicitly requests to speak with a human
- The issue involves potential data loss or security
- The customer expresses strong dissatisfaction (3+ messages)
- The issue is a confirmed bug requiring code fix
- The query involves a refund request

## Self-Hosted vs SaaS Support

**Self-Hosted Products**:
- Community support via documentation and forums
- Email support for bugs and critical issues
- Response times may be longer

**SaaS Products**:
- Full support included with subscription
- Priority support available on higher tiers

## Customer Satisfaction

- Follow up within 24 hours of resolution for Critical and High priority tickets
- Document all escalations for monthly review
- Continuously improve based on feedback and issue patterns

## SLA Exclusions

SLA targets do not apply during:
- Scheduled maintenance (announced in advance)
- Force majeure events
- Issues caused by third-party services
- Circumstances beyond our reasonable control
