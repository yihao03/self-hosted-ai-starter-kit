# Delivery & Access Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-01

## Software Delivery

After purchase, you will receive immediate access to the software via:
- Download link sent to your registered email
- Direct access through your account dashboard
- API keys delivered through the admin panel (for self-hosted deployments)

## License Delivery

Licenses are delivered electronically:
- License key or activation code sent within minutes of purchase
- For subscription plans, access is granted immediately upon payment confirmation
- Physical media is not provided

## Access Issues

If you cannot access your purchased software:
- Check your spam/junk folder for delivery emails
- Log into your account dashboard to retrieve license keys
- Contact support within 48 hours if you have not received access

## Account Creation

To access the software, you must create an account. You are responsible for:
- Maintaining the security of your account credentials
- All activity that occurs under your account

## Refund Eligibility

Software and digital products are eligible for refund only under the following conditions:
- The software fails to function as described
- You are unable to access the product within 72 hours of purchase
- Technical issues prevent installation or use

See our [Return & Refund Policy](return-policy.md) for full details.
