# Return & Refund Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-01

## Overview

All sales of software products are final. Due to the digital nature of our products, we generally do not offer refunds once the software has been downloaded, installed, or accessed.

## Eligibility for Refund

We may consider refunds only in the following circumstances:

- **Technical Failure**: The software fails to function as advertised and we are unable to resolve the issue within 30 days
- **Non-Delivery**: You have not received access to the software within 72 hours of purchase (excluding scheduled maintenance)
- **Accidental Purchase**: For self-hosted products, you may request a refund within 14 days if you have not downloaded, installed, or activated the software

## Non-Refundable Items

The following are non-refundable:
- Downloaded or installed software
- Activated licenses or API keys
- Subscription fees (pro-rated or full)
- Custom integrations or customizations
- Professional services (consulting, setup, training)
- Any product marked as "final sale"

## Process for Refund Requests

1. Contact our support team with your order number and reason for request
2. Provide any relevant documentation (error logs, screenshots)
3. We will respond within 3 business days
4. If approved, refunds are processed within 5-7 business days

## Subscription Cancellations

- You may cancel your subscription at any time
- Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period
- No refunds are provided for partial billing periods
- Access to premium features continues until the end of the billing period

## Exchange Policy

We do not offer exchanges for software products. If you experience issues:
- Contact support for technical assistance
- We will work to resolve any bugs or compatibility issues

## Chargebacks

Initiating a chargeback or dispute for a valid transaction may result in:
- Suspension or termination of your account
- Loss of access to all purchased products
- Future purchase restrictions

## Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven't received a refund after 7 business days:
1. Check your bank account again
2. Contact your credit card company (it may take time to post)
3. Contact your bank
4. If you've done all of this and still have not received your refund, contact us at support@example.com
